# 03 · Telling Time · *La hora*

---

## The basics: *es* vs *son*

> Use **es la** for 1:00 (singular). Use **son las** for everything else (plural).

| Time | Spanish |
|------|---------|
| 1:00 | **Es la** una |
| 2:00 | **Son las** dos |
| 12:00 | **Son las** doce |

---

## On the hour · *En punto*

| Time | Spanish |
|------|---------|
| 1:00 | Es la una **en punto** |
| 3:00 | Son las tres **en punto** |
| 8:00 | Son las ocho **en punto** |

> *En punto* means "sharp" / "exactly." You can leave it out in casual speech.

---

## Minutes past · *y*

> Add **y + minutes** after the hour. Used from :01 to :30.

| Time | Spanish | English |
|------|---------|---------|
| 2:05 | Son las dos **y cinco** | five past two |
| 4:10 | Son las cuatro **y diez** | ten past four |
| 7:20 | Son las siete **y veinte** | twenty past seven |
| 9:25 | Son las nueve **y veinticinco** | twenty-five past nine |

---

## Quarter and half · *y cuarto / y media*

| Time | Spanish | English |
|------|---------|---------|
| 12:15 | Son las doce **y cuarto** | quarter past twelve |
| 6:15 | Son las seis **y cuarto** | quarter past six |
| 12:30 | Son las doce **y media** | half past twelve |
| 3:30 | Son las tres **y media** | half past three |

---

## Minutes to · *para las*

> In Latin America, use **[minutes] para las [next hour]** for times after :30.
> This is the opposite of *y* — you count forward to the next hour.

| Time | Spanish | English |
|------|---------|---------|
| 11:35 | **Veinticinco para las** doce | twenty-five to twelve |
| 11:40 | **Veinte para las** doce | twenty to twelve |
| 11:45 | **Un cuarto para las** doce | quarter to twelve |
| 11:50 | **Diez para las** doce | ten to twelve |
| 11:55 | **Cinco para las** doce | five to twelve |

> *Un cuarto para las doce* = it's 15 minutes **until** twelve = 11:45.

---

## Special times

| Spanish | English |
|---------|---------|
| el mediodía | noon (12:00 pm) |
| la medianoche | midnight (12:00 am) |
| Son las doce del mediodía | It is noon |
| Es la medianoche | It is midnight |

---

## Morning, afternoon, night · *de la mañana / tarde / noche*

> Spanish has no "am/pm" — use these phrases instead.

| Spanish | Used for | Example |
|---------|----------|---------|
| de la **mañana** | morning (roughly 6am–12pm) | Son las ocho de la mañana — 8:00 am |
| de la **tarde** | afternoon (roughly 12pm–8pm) | Son las tres de la tarde — 3:00 pm |
| de la **noche** | night (roughly 8pm–midnight) | Son las diez de la noche — 10:00 pm |

---

## Asking and answering

| Spanish | English |
|---------|---------|
| ¿Qué hora es? | What time is it? |
| ¿A qué hora…? | At what time…? |
| Es la una | It is one o'clock |
| Son las cinco y media | It is half past five |
| A las nueve y cuarto | At quarter past nine |
