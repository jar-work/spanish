## Tens · *Las decenas*

> From 31 onward, use **two words joined by *y***: *treinta **y** uno*, *treinta **y** dos*, etc.

| # | Spanish | English |
|---|---------|---------|
| 40 | cuarenta | forty |
| 50 | cincuenta | fifty |
| 60 | sesenta | sixty |
| 70 | setenta | seventy |
| 80 | ochenta | eighty |
| 90 | noventa | ninety |
| 100 | cien | one hundred |

---

## 101, 102 …

> **100 alone** = *cien*. **100 + something** = *ciento*: *ciento uno*, *ciento dos* …

| # | Spanish | English |
|---|---------|---------|
| 101 | ciento uno | one hundred and one |
| 102 | ciento dos | one hundred and two |

---

## Hundreds · *Las centenas*
